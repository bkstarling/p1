FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is STRATX Algo?

STRATX is a research-driven systematic intraday index strategy platform that deploys rule-based basket strategies within a structured capital allocation and defined risk framework on NSE and BSE. We do not provide tips or discretionary trading signals. We operate through predefined strategy frameworks.

How is STRATX different from regular trading signals?

STRATX does not provide tips or discretionary calls. All strategies operate within predefined rules, structured risk parameters, and disciplined execution logic. Decisions are rule-driven and executed within a defined framework, not based on market opinion or emotion.

How do I use StratX Algos?

Choose the algo from the list and save it. Then follow these steps:

  1. Validate: Analyze past performance including brokerage, slippages, taxes and charges.
  2. Forward Test: Test the algo with virtual capital first.
  3. Activate: Activate the algo between 8:30 AM and 9:15 AM only. Late activation may alter algo logic.
  4. Specified Days Only: Activate only on the specified days mentioned in the algo card.
  5. Go Live: Login with your broker and automate real trades in your account.
